Fifth planet from the Sun and largest planet in the solar system. Got over 80 orbiting moons and possibly many more. Named after the Roman god Jupiter, the king of the gods.
Pioneer 10 was the first spacecraft to visit Jupiter, making its closest approach to the planet in December 1973. In 2007, the New Horizons visited Jupiter using its gravity to increase its speed. Future targets for exploration in the Jupiter system include the probable ice-covered liquid ocean of Europa.
